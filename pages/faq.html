Yearbook FAQs

The yearbook team is always looking for student and faculty candid sumbissions! If you have a photo you'd like to submit, you can email it to our editor jp4858@trevor.org or the yearbook email at yearbook@trevor.org

The deadline for senior page submissions is MM/DD/YY. Please make sure to submit all required materials by this date to ensure your page is included in the yearbook.

You can select your headshot once they are provided by stomping ground. You will receive an email with instructions from no-reply@imagequix.com to your school email adress.

Our team uses names as they appear on the portal. If you would like your preferred name to be used, please ensure you have it set on the portal.

If you have any additional questions, please feel free to reach out to our editor at jp4858@trevor.org.

If you're interested in getting involved with the yearbook team, please reach out to our editor at jp4858@trevor.org. Keep in mind that this is a high engagement club which requires significant time and commitment.

All senior forms will be posted on this page. You will also receive an email from yearbook@trevor.org when they are available.

All photo retouching is provided by stomping ground. Once you recieve your gallery you must purcahce any print version of your headshot. When checking out you will have the option to pay an additonal fee for retouching.
